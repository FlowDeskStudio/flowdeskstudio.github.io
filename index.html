Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before buying.

What software do I need?

Microsoft Excel 2016 or newer, or Microsoft 365 (Windows or Mac). Core formulas also work in Google Sheets, though some formatting may differ.

Are macros required?

No. Every template uses pure Excel formulas only. No macros, no VBA, no security warnings. Just open and start entering your data.

Can I customize the categories and fields?

Yes. Income categories, expense categories, project statuses, client statuses, and priority levels are all fully editable.

Is sample data included?

Yes. Each workbook comes with realistic sample data so you can see exactly how everything works. Delete it when you're ready to start fresh.

Can I use this for multiple years?

Yes. Change the year in the Setup sheet and all monthly calculations adjust automatically. Or duplicate the workbook for each year.

Do the two workbooks connect to each other?

They work independently. Each has its own Setup page and functions as a standalone tool. This keeps things simple and avoids cross-file dependency issues.

What if I only need one template?

Each product works perfectly on its own. The bundle simply saves you $4 if you want both. There's no pressure to buy more than you need.
